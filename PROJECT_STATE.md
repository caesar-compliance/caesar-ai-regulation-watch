## Product vision (current)

Global AI regulation monitoring product:

- Global map / globe and jurisdiction profiles
- Official source registry with credibility tiers
- Law and guidance records, timelines, change history
- Status labels and review-gated AI summaries
- Affected controls and affected evidence mapping
- RSS / JSON / evidence export and public website
- Future Caesar AI Governance OS regulatory inbox

**Not claimed:** legal advice, compliance guarantees, or complete global coverage.

Full detail: [docs/FULL_SCALE_PRODUCT_BLUEPRINT.md](docs/FULL_SCALE_PRODUCT_BLUEPRINT.md)
